📱 Phone-only? Use Myfxbook cloud (no desktop MT5)

The MT5 phone app cannot connect to this dashboard directly. The easiest path if you only trade on your phone is Myfxbook — it links to XM in the cloud and pulls every trade you place on mobile.

  1. Free account at myfxbook.com
  2. Add Account → MetaTrader 5 → broker XM → your account number
  3. Use your investor (read-only) password — not your main trading password
  4. In config.json: set myfxbook_email, myfxbook_password, then click List accounts below to get myfxbook_account_id

Alternative: log into the same XM account on desktop MT5 (minimized) and use Sync MT5.
